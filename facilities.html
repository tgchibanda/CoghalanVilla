FACILITIES

  • GYM
  • LOUNGE

Our Accommodation comprises of 4 Executive en-suite Rooms, 4 fully equipped spacious Self Catering Suites and 22 Standard En-suite rooms. Tea and coffee making facilities are available in each room. There’s wireless broadband internet access, a 32” LCD TV which is full DSTV together with all modern amenities to make your stay as comfortable and memorable. The rooms are serviced daily and we offer laundry service to our guests. Dry cleaning can be arranged for the convenience of our guests.

A mini gym is on site for the exclusive use of our guests.

A big guest lounge is available for the use of guests, where they can relax, curl on the couch to enjoy a good book, network with other guests or watch TV, read book or watch the birds from the verandah. During the winter months and colder days guests can sit by the fireplaces to enjoy a good book and unwind after a busy day.

The garden is dotted with reading spots, picnic tables with shades where guests can bask in the sun, sit in the shade to enjoy the birds, read a book, catch up with paper work and so much more. It is a little paradise, a home away from home. During the summers the sparkling swimming pool is a delight to jump in for a cool dip, or just lounge by the pool, sipping a drink and enjoying the afternoon or early evening breeze.

Two 15 Seater and 30 Seater conference rooms are available and fully equipped, with modern amenities. A quiet and tranquil environment for those important brain storming sessions.
